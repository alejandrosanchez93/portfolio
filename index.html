About Me

My name is Alejandro Sanchez, I am 27 years old, and I am currently enrolled in a three month boot camp. We are in week two and things are definitely getting spicy.

I was raised in the Rio Grande Valley, currently reside in McAllen, and have been here most of my life. I have traveled to different states and cities working jobs from retail to working in fabrication shops. I have never been more excited about learning a new skill set. I was a pipe welder for two years and that was fun to learn, but this boot camp has been a more fulfilling experience so far.

When I am not working or studying I like to spend my time with my pets. I have a three year old shih tsu named Goku, a four year old pitbull named Bruiser, a six year old ball python named Leonidas, and last but not least an eight year old orange tabby named Simba. My hobbies also consist of playing video games, drawing, skateboarding, playing guitar and ocassionally the keyboard/piano.
